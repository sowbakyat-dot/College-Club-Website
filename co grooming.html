Grooming Club
Grooming & Personality Development

The Grooming Club focuses on developing confidence, communication skills, and personality among students. Through various sessions and workshops, members learn about self-presentation, professional etiquette, leadership qualities, and overall personal development.

The club organizes interactive activities such as mock interviews, soft-skill seminars, fashion grooming, and public speaking events to help students express themselves with confidence and grace.

“Confidence is the best outfit — rock it and own it.”
